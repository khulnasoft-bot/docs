Through Teleflow's web dashboard, you have the power to effortlessly tailor your brand settings, streamlining the process of creating notifications. Customize key elements such as your logo, which will be seamlessly integrated into your email communications.

For those utilizing Teleflow's In-App notification center, the ability to configure the primary font and its corresponding color awaits you. This empowers you to craft an experience for your users that resonates with your unique brand identity.
