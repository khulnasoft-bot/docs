The Teleflow dashboard features an integrated template store for workflows, offering a range of ready-to-use options. 

From basic alerts to intricate sequences, you can select a template, customize it, and swiftly trigger notifications tailored to your needs. 
This streamlined process cuts down development time and empowers you to communicate effectively with minimal effort.
