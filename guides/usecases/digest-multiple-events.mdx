---
title: "Digest multiple events in a single notification"
description: "Learn to leverage Teleflow in digesting multiple events."
---

One of the recurring features developers yearn for is the ability to batch multiple notifications and send as one to subscribers.

Teleflow ships with a built-in digest engine that handles batching and digesting multiple events in a single notification. You can programmatically adjust the digest recurrence or set it from the dashboard.
